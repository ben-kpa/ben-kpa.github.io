A Unified Place For A Gold Standard Workflow

1. Take Notes
Increase your note throughput by taking them right in the cloud from anywhere. You only need a laptop and internet access. Share your notebooks with your study buddies to take them together. Discuss their main points and agree on what you should start learning for the next step.
2. Create Flashcards
Testing yourself on high yield notes is essential for passing an exam and that's what flashcards are for. Extend their usefulness by never searching again for explanations you already took notes on. Save more time for actual review by keeping your flashcards linked to their original notes, and order them the way you want.
3. Review Flashcards
Choose the review style depending on your personality and available time. Use the flashcard-tester with custom difficulties. Go over them linearly. Review them all once first, before you re-review the more difficult ones. If you prefer to test yourself on paper, print them in a "question-only" format and have easy access to the answers online. For a fast passed review use the "hover-to-reveal" option to quickly review them all once more, before you ace that exam.
